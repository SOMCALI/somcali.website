Chocolae Chip Cookie recipe

ingrediets

ingredients
  • 1 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups flour, unsifted
  • 1 tea spoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup walnut or pecans, chopped (optional)
  • 2 cups kcc bittersweet 60% cocao baking chips

baking instruction

  1. heat oven to 375 degree F.
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt;setaside.
  3. Inlarge mixing bowl,eat butter with sugar, and rown.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time. mix on low speed.
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamer mixture.
  6. stir.Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie cookie sheets.
  7. bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chip cooked.
  8. Enjoy!
